Our Story

Welcome to Wild Grove Organic Farm & Resort

Madhu Ravindran, after dedicating two decades to a corporate career in IT Security, made a transformative decision to pursue his deep-seated passion for nature and simplicity. Embracing a profound love for the natural world and animals, he established Wild Grove Organic Farm and Resort, a sanctuary that embodies tranquility and sustainable living.

Located in Sholayur, this resort offers guests an opportunity to reconnect with nature, surrounded by the gentle hum of windmills and nestled within a lush organic farm.

Madhu's vision is to share the serenity and fulfillment of rural life with others, providing an escape from the hustle and bustle of urban existence. His journey is wholeheartedly supported by his wife, Smitha, who continues her corporate role while contributing invaluable ideas and moral support to the venture.

Smitha aspires to join Madhu at the farm full-time, reflecting their shared commitment to this harmonious lifestyle.

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit to Wild Grove.

How far is Wild Grove Resort from Coimbatore? ▼
Wild Grove Resort is located in Sholayur, Attapadi — approximately 1.5 hour's drive from Coimbatore and just 20 minutes from Anaikatti. The route passes through scenic mountain roads with windmill views. We can provide detailed driving directions or arrange a pickup from Coimbatore on request.
What are the check-in and check-out times? ▼
Check-in: 2:00 PM  |  Check-out: 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available on request, subject to availability. Please contact us in advance if you need flexible timings.
Are meals included in the stay? ▼
We offer delicious farm-to-table Kerala-style meals prepared with organic produce from our own farm. Meal plans can be added to your booking. Our specialties include traditional Kerala breakfast (puttu, appam, dosa), lunch sadya, and home-style dinner. Vegetarian and non-vegetarian options are available.
Is the resort suitable for children? ▼
Absolutely! Wild Grove is very family-friendly. Kids love the organic farming class (planting and harvesting), swimming pool, nature walks, bonfire evenings, and spotting birds and butterflies. We also have open spaces where children can play freely. The off-road and night safari are family-friendly adventures too.
What should I pack for my stay? ▼
We recommend packing: comfortable walking shoes/trekking sandals, light layers (evenings can be cool), sunscreen & hat, mosquito repellent, camera, swimwear for the pool, and a light rain jacket (during monsoon months). The resort provides towels, toiletries, and bedding.
Do you have WiFi and mobile connectivity? ▼
Yes, we offer free WiFi in common areas. Mobile connectivity is available (BSNL/Jio work best in this area). However, we encourage guests to disconnect and enjoy the natural surroundings — that's the Wild Grove way! 🌿
What is the cancellation policy? ▼
Free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in for a full refund. Cancellations within 3-7 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 72 hours of check-in are non-refundable. We recommend booking early, especially for weekends and holiday seasons, as availability is limited.
Is parking available? ▼
Yes! We provide free private parking within the resort premises. The resort is accessible by car — the last stretch is a scenic mountain road. We can provide driving directions with landmarks to make navigation easy.
Getting Here

How to Reach Wild Grove

Located in Sholayur, Attapadi — surrounded by the Western Ghats.

By Air

Coimbatore International Airport (CJB) — 65 km away, ~1.5 hours by road. Taxis and cab services available at the airport.

By Train

Coimbatore Junction (CBE) — Well-connected to Chennai, Bangalore, Kochi. From the station, hire a cab (~1.5 hours drive).

By Road

From Coimbatore: ~60 km via Anaikatti Road → Sholayur. Well-maintained scenic route. From Kochi: ~180 km (~4 hours). From Bangalore: ~430 km (~7 hours).
